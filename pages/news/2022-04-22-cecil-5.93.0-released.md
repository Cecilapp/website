### Features

#### New template filter: `hex_to_rgb`

The [`hex_to_rgb` filter](/documentation/templates/#hex-to-rgb) converts a hexadecimal color to RGB.

_Usage example:_

```twig
{{ '#163C56'|hex_to_rgb }}
```
