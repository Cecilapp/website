### Features

#### JSON Feed output format

Add support of [JSON Feed](https://www.jsonfeed.org/) as output format.

```yaml
output:
  pagetypeformats:
    section: ['html', 'atom', 'jsonfeed']
```

Documentation: <https://cecil.app/documentation/configuration/#pagetypeformats>
